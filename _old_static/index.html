About Us

A Legacy of
Academic Excellence

Unilorin Secondary School was established in 1982 following the approval of the University of Ilorin Council in November 1981. Official approval came via letter PLAN/GEN/9/T/93 on June 21, 1982, and the school was inaugurated on May 17, 1982, by the then Vice Chancellor, Professor Afolabi Toye.

Originally established to cater for the educational needs of children and wards of University staff, the school has grown into a prestigious institution serving the broader Ilorin community and beyond. Today, USS operates as an autonomous, self-financing community service outreach venture of the University of Ilorin.

Our Mission

To establish a nurturing and challenging environment where students excel academically and grow holistically.

Our Vision

A model centre of academic excellence producing disciplined, well-rounded graduates ready to lead.

Academics

Comprehensive Education

Our curriculum is designed to develop the whole student — intellectually, morally, and physically.

01

Sciences

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics taught with modern laboratory resources and practical approaches.

02

Humanities

English Language, Literature, History, Government, and Social Studies fostering critical thinking and expression.

03

Technology

Computer Science, Information Technology, and Digital Literacy preparing students for the modern world.

04

Languages

English, Yoruba, French, and Arabic — building communication skills and cultural awareness.
